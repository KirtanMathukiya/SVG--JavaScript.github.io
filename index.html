Car Dodge Challenge

In "Car Dodge Challenge," you use the arrow keys(up and down) on your computer to drive a tiny green car. Avoiding collisions with the red obstacles in your path is your primary objective. Move the car up and down to avoid the obstacles by using your skills. You gain a point for each one you manage to dodge. But take caution! The game is over if you run into an obstruction. Simply click the start button to start the game or stop button to end the game. Check out your score by deftly avoiding the obstacles in this enjoyable and difficult game!
